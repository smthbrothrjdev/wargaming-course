# What is wargaming
A dynamic representation of **conflict or competition** in which **people make decisions** and respond to the **consequences** of those decisions.

## Advantages
 - Safe to fail
 - Simulated

## Purpose
 ### Eductational

  its for students

  **purpose**: reinforce the learning objectives

**requires**: knowing learning outcome (know the answer)

### Experientional
  
  its for the students.


**purpose**: to practice.

**requires**: knowing training objectives.

### Analytical

 its for the sponsor

**purpose**: Extract insights to identify gaps and challenges.

**requires**: requires expertise from players and understand the sponsor's problem.

## 5 phases
1. Initiate
2. Design (linked)
3. Develop (linked)
4. Conduct
