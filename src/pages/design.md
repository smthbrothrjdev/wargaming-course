# Design notes

## Open vs Closed

#### Open 
 All information about the game is viewable by the participants

#### Closed
 Some or all information about the game is not viewable by one or more participants.

## Constraints, Limitations, and Assumptions

- Specify the impact on quality of the wargame

### Constraint
- A restriction imposed **by the sponsor** that limits the study team's options in conducting the study.
- Examples are time, location, scenario that must be used.

### Limitation
- An inability **of the team** to fully meet the study objectives or fully investigate the study issues.

### Assumption
- A statement related to the study that is taken as true in the absence of facts, often **often to accomodate a limitation**

### Five Tenets of assumptions
1. Full vs Key
2. Develop in order
3. Criteria: Necessary, Valid, **Accepted**
4. Continually review and update)
5. Tailor to audience 

### Sources
- Sponsor
- Wargaming team (when focusing or constraining the players operating within the published rules)
- Wargaming team (when accomodating a limitation)
